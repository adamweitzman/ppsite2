PRODUCT

Perception Point’s advanced email protection blocks any exploit executed via the email.

Today 90% of targeted attacks occur through email, the most common gateway into an organization. Our e-mail solution blocks any exploit executed via this channel. All messages are “x-rayed” before they arrive at the user’s inbox, providing full visibility and prevention of malicious attachments and links, to pre-empt the triggering of application bugs. Cleaner data and stronger engines allow for shorter analysis times, so users experience zero security delays when opening messages. With one-click activation and seamless integration, our email solution immediately forties the organization against the most damaging attacks.
